OPEN NOW EVENT

 

On the first day of Hanukkah, the celebration of light in the midst of the darkest days of the year, we gathered to celebrate the inauguration of the first All Nations Café

Participants came from different nations and from three sister religions: Islam, Christianity and Judaism. We had enthusiastic Israelis, Palestinians, Arab Israelis, many beautiful Germans and Americans, and wonderful people from Czech Republic, West Africa and South America. 

We had a tri-lingual live concert by Musicians for Peace with Jewish, Arab and German musicians who volunteered their services (you can hear them now in the background). The audience joined in the singing and dancing and the energy went up high to the sky.

We lit the Hanukkah candles, symbolizing the small light that dispels all darkness. We also held an energy circle and a blessing for peace for all nations and for the All Nations Celebrations to spread all over the world. Middle Eastern food (falafel), raw food (ReekaSalad) and special drinks (Magic Sakhleb, Turkish Coffee and Herbal Teas) were served free of charge to every guest. They were made by a team including a Muslim Sheik and his four kids, two Palestinian families from the Mount of Olives, a German chef and Israeli Jews.

We wish to thank God and to thank everybody who helped clean, drive, carry, decorate, prepare, feed, finance, sing, play and all of you who made this event possible. We Love You !!

Hope to see you next month at the All Nations Celebration.
