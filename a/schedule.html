HOW TO GET THERE
Abed's Land is located on the Walajah Road from Malha train station in south west Jerusalem to Beit Jala and Bethlehem. A couple of kms after crossing the railway line (and then passing by Ein Ya'el) you reach an army checkpoint -- pass through it and you will see a dirt road immediately on your left, climbing up for a few hundred meters, until you see a shaded area in the midst of a well cultivated plot -- this is the place!
