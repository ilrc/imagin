PLANTING a FUTURE

Now, wherever you are in the world, you can still take a part in the All Nations Café work and make a sustainable contribution to the environment by sponsoring planting a tree at the Grove of All Nations on the land between Jerusalem and Bethlehem.

We plant the young trees during the rainy season (November-February) to allow them ample supply of water. We protect each tree from deer and other animals and nurture it through its first year until it is strong enough. In just few years the new tree bears fruits and as it grows it provides shade and protection from wind and erosion. Throughout its life, it produces oxygen and reduces carbon dioxide levels in the atmosphere.
