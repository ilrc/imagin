IMAGIN Online Gathering

We invite you to a special "Imagin" event on April 18th at 19:00, where we will connect across countries around camp fires and candles. We encourage you to get outdoors if you can, to a wild place, a garden, a balcony or a courtyard. Light a fire, a candle or a lamp, and join us on zoom.

Imagin is an initiative of Elders and Youth aimed to create a safe space for young people from the Middle East and Europe, away from the educational-economic-cultural system, where they can explore their true purpose and find role models of true adulthood.
